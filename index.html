How it works

Your data stays in
your building. Always.

Aureq's node software runs on your own infrastructure. We have no access to your transactions. We never see your customer records.

01

Train locally on your own data

Each institution runs an Aureq node on their own servers. The fraud model trains entirely on your own transaction history. Nothing leaves your network.

ZERO DATA MOVEMENT
02

Share only what was learned

After training, nodes exchange encrypted model weights — statistical fraud signatures, not transactions. Validated against manipulation before being applied to any model.

ENCRYPTED · PEER-TO-PEER
03

Every institution improves

Your fraud model improves from what every other institution on the network has seen — without any of their data reaching you, or yours reaching them.

REAL-TIME NETWORK EFFECT
04

Your rules engine stays in control

Aureq returns a risk score to your existing decision system. You approve, flag, or block — using your own thresholds. Aureq is never in the decision loop.
